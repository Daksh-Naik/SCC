Sufficient care is undertaken to make the slogan look flawless. The slogan may look different on various devices. Medium sized Laptops or Desktops render the slogan accurately. The slogan (hand, vote-box and fictional candidates) is purely made from CSS and is NOT an image. So it's evident for the fact that the slogan will have different sizes across all devices. Hence, trial-and-error method is often used to render the slogan precisely.

There was a way of rendering the slogan by image / SVG obviously, but in order to make it a fair-play and to get some creativity points, simple CSS is preferred for the same.


The prime motive or the significance of the design of this website centres around the idea of having the Aesthetic and Focussed feel, just like other websites when it comes to designing the landing page. It may seem empty, compared to other slogans, but the deserted feel of the slogan is intentional. Having a plethora of sentences in and around the slogan to maximize interaction doesn't matter when it comes to Slogans which are drawn by hand but if it were to implement so many sentences on / around the slogan on web, the design would loose its aestheticness because of the cluster, which will make it look unpleasant. This would even loose the focussed feel of the main component of the slogan. Added to this, this would even compromise in maintaining Media Responsiveness across all devices. So, rounding all this up, the empty spaces around the slogan is intentional and so the cluster is avoided.


Website / Webpage is hosted by Github. I have nothing to do with the web server. All I have taken care of is the Front End and UX. Decision, planning, UX / UI, artwork has been done by me.

Design

The prime motive or the significance of the design of this website centres around the idea of having the Aesthetic and Focussed feel, just like other websites when it comes to designing the landing page. It may seem empty, compared to other slogans, but the deserted feel of the slogan is intentional. Having a plethora of sentences in and around the slogan to maximize interaction doesn't matter when it comes to Slogans which are drawn by hand. If it were to implement so many sentences on the slogan on web, the design would loose its aestheticness because of the cluster, which will make it look unpleasant. This would even loose the focussed feel of the main component of the slogan. Added to this, this would even compromise in maintaining Media Responsiveness across all devices. So, rounding all this up, the empty spaces around the slogan is intentional and so the cluster is avoided.
